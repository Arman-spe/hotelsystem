Franchises History

Jaipur

The very first AARC Hotel opened its doors in Jaipur in the year 2020, marking the beginning of a new era in boutique luxury hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Rajasthan's capital, this flagship property quickly gained recognition for its elegant design, personalized service, and authentic cultural experiences — setting the foundation for the AARC brand's future growth.

A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia. It is a paradisematic country, in which roasted parts of sentences fly into your mouth.

Udaipur

Following the success of its flagship property in Jaipur, AARC expanded its presence with the opening of its second hotel in Udaipur in 2022. Located near the serene lakes and historic palaces, the Udaipur property quickly became a favorite among travelers seeking a blend of luxury, tradition, and scenic beauty. With its elegant architecture and exceptional guest experience, AARC Udaipur continues the brand’s commitment to delivering timeless hospitality.

Agra

AARC continued its journey of redefining boutique luxury with the launch of its Udaipur hotel in 2022, offering guests a tranquil escape surrounded by lakes and heritage. Building on this momentum, AARC proudly opened its third property in Agra in 2024, just minutes away from the iconic Taj Mahal.
